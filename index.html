About

Stephen Parsons

Stephen Parsons is a Christian singer-songwriter whose music is rooted in the honest moments — the ones you only share with the people you love most. Songs about daughters and mothers, grief and gratitude, coming home and staying close.

His debut album Touches of the Heart is a collection of deeply personal songs written from lived experience. No performance, no pretense — just faith, family, and the kind of truth that doesn't need to be dressed up.

Released through Hello Texas Records, Stephen Parsons brings a solo voice to stories that connect where they were always meant to: in the quiet places, at the kitchen table, and in the moments that stay with you forever.
